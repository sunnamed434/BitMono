# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you find a security vulnerability in BitMono, please **do not open a public issue**.
Email **hello@bitmono.dev** instead.

Where possible, include:

- a description of the issue and its impact,
- steps to reproduce (a proof of concept helps a lot),
- the BitMono version / commit and your environment.

We'll acknowledge your report as soon as we can, keep you updated on the fix, and credit you
(if you want) once it's resolved. Please give us a reasonable chance to ship a fix before
disclosing publicly.

## Scope

BitMono is a free, open-source obfuscator provided as-is. Reports about the obfuscator and its
CLI/library, as well as the **bitmono.dev** website, are all welcome at the address above.
